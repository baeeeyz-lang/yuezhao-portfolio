Protection Reimagined

01

从界面问题到系统挑战 From interface problem to system challenge

最初的假设是：用户不理解保险，是因为语言和流程太复杂。研究后发现，真正的问题同时受到监管、 定价、风险评估、理赔逻辑和脆弱信任关系的影响。

The first assumption was that protection insurance only needed simpler language. Research revealed a larger service challenge shaped by regulation, pricing, risk assessment, claims logic and fragile trust.

02

从抗拒购买到缺少相关性 From resistance to relevance

访谈显示，用户并非单纯排斥保险，而是很难在当下生活中感到它与自己有关。 只有当家庭责任、人生阶段或财务风险变得可见，保障才开始有意义。

Interviews showed that people were not simply avoiding insurance. Protection often felt distant until family responsibility, life stage or financial risk became personally visible.

03

从销售转化到支持式理解 From transaction to support

追访案例暴露出：强销售感、保障内容不清晰和家庭支出压力，会让用户取消保单并长期回避再次了解。

A follow-up interview exposed how sales pressure, unclear cover and household cost pressure can lead to cancellation and long-term avoidance.

服务介入点 Service intervention

用户信心从哪里开始崩塌 Where confidence begins to collapse

设计问题从“如何增加信息”转向“用户从好奇到被迫决策的断点在哪里”。机会点不是再堆叠说明， 而是在浏览与报价之间建立一个低压力的支持层。

The key design question shifted from adding more information to finding the point where curiosity turns into decision pressure. The opportunity became a supportive bridge between browsing and quote logic.

  1. 搜索Search 产生好奇Curiosity
  2. 浏览Browse 信息碎片化Fragmented information
  3. 比较Compare 过早决策压力Premature pressure
  4. 暂停Pause 不确定感Uncertainty
  5. 离开Exit 流失Drop-off
设计回应 Design response

整合式支持层 Integrated support layer

最终方案把“低压力问题探索空间”和“流程型旅程引导”结合起来：先帮助用户在进入报价前理解保障， 再在决策过程中保持流程可见。

The final proposal combines a low-pressure exploration space with process-based journey guidance. It helps users understand protection before the quote journey, then keeps the process visible as they move towards a decision.

Prototype 1 问题式探索空间 Question-Based Exploration Space

让用户在没有表单、比较压力和即时承诺的情况下理解保障。

Understand protection without forms, comparison pressure or immediate commitment.

Prototype 2 流程型旅程引导 Process-Based Journey Guide

让报价、保障、付款与理赔步骤更可预期。

Make quote, cover, payment and claim steps easier to anticipate.

最终方向 Final 负责任的清晰 Responsible clarity

用白话和视觉解释降低理解门槛，但不掩盖金融服务中必要的复杂性。

Use plain language and visual explanation without hiding regulated complexity.

这个项目的长期价值，是学习如何让复杂系统变得可理解，而不是把它简单化。 The long-term value of this project is learning to make complex systems understandable without making them simplistic.
